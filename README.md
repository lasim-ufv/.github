# Welcome to the GitHub of the Molecular Simulation Lab!

## About us

The Molecular Simulation Lab (LaSIM) is located at the University of Viçosa (Brazil) and led by prof. Marcelo D. Polêto. Our group uses computational modeling and simulations to study enzymes to delevop the next-generation biocatalytic solutions for polymer degradation and bioremediation. Our mission is to to unlock the full potential of enzymes for biotechnological applications and drive the transition toward a truly circular and sustainable bioeconomy.
